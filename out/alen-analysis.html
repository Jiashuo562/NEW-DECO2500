# Gender Distribution & Frequency
gender_counts = df["gender"].value_counts()
gender_proportion = df["gender"].value_counts(normalize=True) * 100

# Distribution
bar_chart(
    categories=gender_counts.index,
    category_freq= list(gender_counts.values),
    title="Gender Distribution"
) 

# Summary/Frequency
create_freq_table("Gender Frequency Table", gender_counts, gender_proportion)

Demographic Analysis¶

From the above we arrive at the following:

  1. Surveyees were not primarily from one gender group and was roughly equal
  2. A significant amount of surveyees were university students, which was naturally expected as we mainly asked around for surveyees in school
  3. Unsurprisingly the largest age-group surveyed coincides with the expected age-group of current university students
  4. About half of the surveyed no longer use wearables. However, their willigness to participate in the survey hints to possible unmet needs that caused them to no longer use wearables
